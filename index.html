About Me

My name is Thanmaya K. Anoop, and I am a high school student with a passion for creativity, technology, and entrepreneurship. Inspired by the dedication and creativity of others, I started my own nail business, Nails By Maya, as a way to express my artistic abilities and share my passion for nail design with others.

Through this journey, I have learned the importance of creativity, attention to detail, and creating meaningful experiences for others. Along with my passion for nail art, I have developed a strong interest in computer science and web development.

I decided to create this website as a way to combine my creativity, technical skills, and entrepreneurial goals into one platform. Nails By Maya represents my journey of learning, growing, and bringing my passions in art, business, and technology together.
